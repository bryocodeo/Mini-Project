Breaking down the stigma of Mental Illness

#MentalHealthAwareness

Smile

For those fighting the battle right now, find something, someone, anything that can make you smile. For those who are fine, smile at people or more importantly, be kind. You never know what someone is going through.

Talk

There are people who understand and will be happy to help.

Imagine

Imagine how great life is going to be when you beat this, BECAUSE YOU WILL.

Get Help

Get help, and know that there is zero shame in asking for it.

Move

Literally, move. Don't underestimate for a second the value of activity, movement and exercise for your mental health. It does WONDERS.

Acceptance

There are many people in this world that would not socialize with someone with a serious mental illness. This won't do. Be accepting of all.
